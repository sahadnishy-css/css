Kashmir Great Lakes

The Kashmir Great Lakes refer to a series of stunning alpine lakes in the high-altitude Himalayas, renowned for the scenic and moderately challenging trek that takes trekkers past them. The trek, typically undertaken between July and September, offers breathtaking views of lush meadows, snow-capped peaks, and vibrant wildflowers, and passes through the serene and often-sacred lakes of Vishansar, Kishansar, Gadsar, Satsar, Nundkol, and Gangbal. The experience is a journey through diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage, making it one of India's most popular and fulfilling treks.

Bolivia

Bolivia is a landlocked country in central South America known for its diverse geography, which includes parts of the Andes Mountains, the Amazon rainforest, and the Altiplano plateau. It has a rich cultural heritage with a large indigenous population and a complex political history, reflected in its official name, the Plurinational State of Bolivia.

Colombia

Colombia is a South American country renowned for its vast biodiversity, rich cultural heritage from indigenous, Spanish, and African influences, and diverse geography. It is the fourth-largest country in South America and is bordered by the Caribbean Sea to the north and the Pacific Ocean to the west, with coastlines on both oceans.

ABOUT US

A travel agent is someone who organizes vacation and travel arrangements for clients, including booking their transportation and lodging. While there are no specific requirements to become a travel agent, holding a certification along with a bachelor's degree may create more job opportunities and make you more marketable. Learning about the different options for travel agent certifications can help you decide which one to pursue.

The CTA certification from The Travel Institute is for travel agents who have completed this training program. To qualify for it, you must have at least one year of industry experience or complete the Travel Agent Proficiency (TAP) test, which is a two-hour proctored test that consists of 100 multiple-choice questions. The training program includes courses about office skills, consumer-focused professionalism and specialized travel sales techniques. It also includes electives with options that range from time management to sales of specialized travel packages. To keep the certification, complete relevant continuing education.
